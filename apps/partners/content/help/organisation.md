Your organisation profile is how customers see your brand across Circls — on every venue, event and membership you list. A complete profile builds trust and helps people recognise who they are booking with. This guide covers editing it.

## Where to find it

Go to **Settings → Organisation profile** (the **Edit organisation profile** card). The editor always applies to the organisation currently selected in the top-bar org switcher.

## Logo

Use **Upload logo** to add your brand logo. Accepted formats are **JPEG, PNG and WebP**, up to **10 MB**. A small preview shows the current logo; use **Replace logo** to swap it or **Remove** to clear it. Replacing or removing deletes the previous image from storage.

## About

- **Organisation name** — your public brand name.
- **Description** — a short summary of who you are and what you offer (up to 1000 characters).

## Contact & links

Add a public **contact email** and **phone**, your **website**, and any social handles or URLs (**Instagram**, **Facebook**, **X**, **YouTube**). Leave any field blank to omit it.

## Address

Enter your organisation's structured postal address — address line 1 and 2, city, state, postal code and country.

## Saving

Click **Save changes** to store the profile. You'll see a **Saved** confirmation. Changes take effect immediately and are visible to customers as soon as your listings are live.

## What customers see

Your logo and name appear as a compact "by {organisation}" brand badge on your public venues, events and memberships. The full profile (description, links, contact and address) is available to consumers through the public organisation page. Billing and internal settings are never shown to customers.

## Tips

- A clear **logo** and **name** are the highest-impact fields — they appear on every listing.
- Keep **contact** details current so customers can reach the right place.
- Switch organisations from the top-bar org switcher if you manage more than one; the editor follows your selection.
