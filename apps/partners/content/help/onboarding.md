Welcome to circls! This guide walks you through the onboarding wizard — from creating your organisation to releasing your first bookable slots. The whole flow takes about five minutes.

## Before you start

You'll need a circls Partner account. If a colleague invited you, follow your invite link instead — you'll join their organisation directly and can skip step 1 (see [Inviting and managing team members](/help/team)).

## Step 1 — Create your organisation

Your **organisation** is the top-level account that owns all of your venues, events, bookings and team members.

1. Enter an **Organisation name** (for example, *Greenfield Sports Club*).
2. A **URL slug** is suggested automatically from the name. You can edit it — it may contain only lowercase letters, numbers and hyphens, and must be unique. If it's taken, you'll see *"That URL slug is already taken — try another."*
3. Click **Create & continue**.

Your organisation is created immediately and you become its **owner**. This is the only step you can't skip — everything else can be added later.

## Step 2 — Add your first venue

A **venue** is a physical location (a club, a complex, a studio).

1. Enter a **Venue name** (for example, *Greenfield Main Campus*).
2. Optionally add **tags** (such as `indoor` or `premium`) to describe it.
3. Click **Add venue** — or **Skip for now** if you'd rather set venues up later.

New venues are created with the status **pending review**. The circls team reviews each listing before it becomes visible to consumers. You can add the address, map location and photos afterwards — see [Managing venues and arenas](/help/venues).

## Step 3 — Add your first arena

An **arena** is a bookable space inside a venue — a court, a pool, a turf, a studio room. This step only appears if you created a venue in step 2.

1. Enter an **Arena name** (for example, *Court 1*).
2. Optionally pick a **Sport** (Badminton, Basketball, Cricket, Football, Padel, Pickleball, Squash, Table Tennis, Tennis, Volleyball, or Other). If you leave it blank, circls tries to infer the sport from your tags.
3. Optionally add **tags**.
4. Click **Add arena**.

Like venues, arenas start as **pending review**. Each arena has a default slot length of 60 minutes, which you can change in the schedule builder.

## Step 4 — Set up a schedule

The final step links you to the schedule builder, where you define opening hours and release bookable slots.

- Click **Open schedule builder** to jump straight in, or **Finish — go to dashboard** to do it later.
- If you skipped the arena step, you'll be sent to the dashboard; you can build schedules any time from the Venues page.

For the full walkthrough, see [Setting up schedules and slot pricing](/help/schedule).

## What happens next

After onboarding you land on your **dashboard**, which shows bookings, revenue and occupancy once they start coming in. Your new venue and arenas sit in **pending review** until the circls team approves them — only then do they appear to consumers and become bookable.

While you wait for approval you can keep building: add more venues and arenas, upload photos, configure schedules, and invite your team.
